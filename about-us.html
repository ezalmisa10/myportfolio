about myself

Hello I am a Senior UI/UX Engineer and also playing a role as a Project Manager based in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, with over 15 years of experience in the information technology industry that spans from various disciplines such as web application development to hands-on UX/UI design and front-end development (HTML/CSS/Javascript).

Through the 15-years of career path journey in the information technology industry, I become a passionate UX/UI designer with high proficiencies in user-centred design, visual UI design, front-end development and problem-solving skills.

With those skills and experience, I manage to design a web-based application for the best customer experiences at DagangAsia Net, a solution provider for the Halal industrial related system that serving the certification bodies worldwide.

The user interface solution is produced through the process of initial UX research to understand the problem and user requirements, presentation of the solution with wireframe design and high fidelity interactive mockup, continuous improvement of the customer experience through feedbacks and statistical report analysis and finally the release of the of the production version of the web application.

The web-based application is designed to allow the certification bodies to manage Halal applicants to apply for the Halal certificate for their products so that the Muslim consumers can rest assure the product they buy is certified as Halal according to Islamic law.

Now the application has been successfully delivered to almost 8 certification bodies from different countries worldwide such as UK, USA, Sri Lanka, Philipines, and Japan.
